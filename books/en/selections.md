# Music

## Three rooms, three frequencies

*Current rotation*

The three rooms begin with three records:

- **Heaven** — Daniel Caesar, *Touching God*
- **Earth** — Stevie Wonder, *Isn't She Lovely*
- **Hell** — D'Angelo, *Really Love*

This is a living collection rather than a definitive ranking—music that establishes a room, a temperature, or a way of moving through the day.
