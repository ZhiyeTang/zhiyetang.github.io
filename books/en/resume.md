#### Making 3D scenes representable, understandable, and streamable

*Research · 2022—2025*

During my master's studies, I worked on 3D Gaussian Splatting and volumetric media, building a research pipeline that spans scene representation, coding and compression, and adaptive streaming systems.

**ProGS · First author**  
ProGS introduces a progressive coding method for 3DGS, organizing a scene as an octree and supporting five levels of detail. It achieves more than `140×` compression on representative datasets, with each level decoding in under `100 ms`.

[Read ProGS](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.09703)

**GSStream · First author**  
GSStream combines collaborative viewport prediction with deep-reinforcement-learning-based bitrate adaptation to build a 3DGS volumetric scene streaming system with low-latency delivery and real-time rendering.

[Read GSStream](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.09718)

**RoCo-GS · Co-author**  
Designed for 4D Gaussian Streaming, RoCo-GS balances robust dynamic-scene reconstruction with an ultra-compact representation, reducing average frame size to approximately `70 KB` while retaining real-time decoding.

**SeCo-GS · Co-author**  
SeCo-GS explores semantic feature compression for enhanced 3DGS representations, reducing data size while preserving the information needed for scene understanding.
