# Research

## ProGS: Progressive Coding for 3D Gaussian Splatting

*2026 · arXiv*

ProGS organizes 3D Gaussian Splatting data into an octree structure to enable efficient **progressive coding** under changing bandwidth conditions.

[Read the paper](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.09703)

## RoCo-GS: Robust and Compact Dynamic Scene Representation

*2026 · Under review*

RoCo-GS brings robust reconstruction and ultra-compact representation together for 4D Gaussian streaming, reducing average frame size to approximately `70 KB` while retaining real-time decoding.

## SeCo-GS: Semantic Feature Compression

*2026 · Under review*

SeCo-GS compresses semantic-enhanced 3DGS through learnable masking, subspace vector quantization, and distance-aware regularization while preserving semantic fidelity.

# Experiments

## Geometry-adaptive primitives for tomography

*2026 · IEEE ICME*

A reconstruction framework based on deformable 3D convex polyhedral primitives models sharp boundaries and complex internal structures more naturally than Gaussian representations.

## RetinexGS: 3DGS for low-light scenes

*2026 · IEEE T-MM*

RetinexGS performs illumination-aware decomposition directly during unsupervised 3DGS training, enabling simultaneous scene reconstruction and low-light enhancement.

## GSStream: Volumetric scene streaming

*2026 · arXiv*

GSStream combines collaborative viewport prediction with deep-reinforcement-learning bitrate adaptation for efficient delivery of 3D Gaussian Splatting scenes.

[Read the paper](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.09718)
